What We Carry

Full-Range Trade Supplies
for Jewelry Professionals

Everything the working jeweler or watchmaker needs — sourced from industry-leading manufacturers and stocked in-store in Miami.

Jewelry Tools
Professional-grade pliers, mandrels, saws, hammers, gravers, and bench tools for all fabrication and repair work.
Watch Movements & Batteries
Renata and Murata watch batteries stocked in full range. Swiss and Japanese movements for watchmakers and repair shops.
Casting & Plating Supplies
Investment powders, waxes, crucibles, plating solutions, and finishing chemicals for production and custom casting.
Testing Equipment
Electronic gold and metal testers, diamond testers, refractometers, and gemological instruments for authentication.
Foredom Flex Shafts
Full line of Foredom flexible shaft machines, handpieces, accessories, and replacement parts for high-volume shops.
Gemoro Products
Gemoro steam cleaners, ultrasonic cleaners, and polishing machines — industry standard for retail and wholesale jewelers.

Manufacturers

Foredom
Flexible Shaft Machines
The gold standard in flexible shaft machines since 1922. Foredom's motors and handpieces are the backbone of jewelry workshops worldwide — from engraving to stone setting.
Legor
Alloys & Precious Metals
Italian manufacturer of premium precious metal alloys, master alloys, and solders trusted by professional casters and refiners across the industry.
Krohn
Polishing & Finishing
Leading supplier of polishing compounds, buffs, wheels, and finishing abrasives for jewelry and watchmaking production environments.
Pike
Saw Blades & Files
German-precision saw blades, needle files, and cutting tools — Pike is the professional's choice for tight tolerances and consistent results in metalwork.
Renata & Murata
Watch Batteries
