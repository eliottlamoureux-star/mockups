Our Services

Evidence-based therapy, tailored to you

We specialize in helping adults aged 20 to 55 navigate life's most challenging moments. Every treatment plan is built around your unique experience.

Anxiety and Depression

CBT and integrative approaches to help you manage persistent worry, low mood, and the physical symptoms that come with them.

Trauma and PTSD

Specialized trauma-focused therapy including EMDR to process difficult experiences and reclaim a sense of safety and stability.

Relationship Issues

Navigate conflict, attachment patterns, and communication challenges. Understand how your relationships shape your emotional life.
