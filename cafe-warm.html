The Experience

A neighbourhood café built for staying

We opened in 2017 because {{LOCATION}} deserved a place where the coffee was roasted with intention and the doors stayed open for people who needed somewhere to think, create, or just be. Our Probat roaster fires up on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays — you can smell it from the sidewalk.

Every barista on our team holds an SCA certification. That means your pour-over, your cortado, your flat white — it's made by someone who genuinely cares about extraction times and grind consistency. Grab a bag of our freshly roasted beans on the way out.
