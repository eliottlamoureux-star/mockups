Our Services

Precision timepiece care from battery swaps to full movement restorations — every job handled with expert care and fair pricing.

Watch Crystal Replacement

Scratched, cracked, or fogged crystal? We custom-fit and replace watch crystals for virtually any timepiece brand or model — our specialty for over 40 years.

Watch Repair & Service

From stem & crown repairs to full movement servicing — we diagnose and repair mechanical, quartz, and automatic watches with precision and care.

Battery Replacement

Quick, professional battery replacement for all quartz watches. Proper gasket reseating ensures water resistance is maintained after every battery change.

Watch Restoration

Bring heirlooms and vintage timepieces back to life. We restore cosmetic finish, polish cases and bracelets, and service movements on antique and collector watches.

Band & Bracelet Service

Watch band replacement, bracelet resizing, polishing, and clasp repair. We carry a wide selection of straps and bracelets to match your style perfectly.

Precision Timepiece Work

Rolex, Omega, TAG Heuer, Seiko and beyond — we service luxury and everyday watches with the same standard of care and attention to detail.
