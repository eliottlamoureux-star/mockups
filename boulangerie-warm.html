The Story

A Neighbourhood Bakery That Still Does Things the Hard Way

{{BUSINESS_NAME}} was born in 2011 from a stubborn belief: real bread takes time. Our stone-deck oven — imported from Lyon and rebuilt twice — sits at the heart of everything we do. Every loaf begins as a simple mix of flour, water, salt, and a starter that's been alive for over a decade.

We're not trying to be the biggest bakery in Quebec City. We just want to be the one your family trusts for Saturday morning bread, the one you call when a birthday cake actually matters, and the one that makes {{LOCATION}} smell like home.
