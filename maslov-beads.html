Our Story

65 Years of Jewelry
Expertise

Maslov Beads was born in Johannesburg in 1959. After decades of serving the jewelry-making community in South Africa, the store made Miami its home in 2004 — and has been a cornerstone of Downtown Miami's design district ever since.

For over 20 years in Miami, we've been the destination of choice for jewelry designers, hobbyists, and craftspeople from across South Florida. Our collection spans thousands of semiprecious stone varieties, cultured pearls, crystals, and professional-grade supplies — curated by people who truly love the craft.

What We Offer

Everything for the
Jewelry Maker

From loose stones to professional repair — one store for everything you need.

💎

Beads & Stones

Thousands of semiprecious stone beads in every cut, size, and finish. Amethyst, turquoise, labradorite, lapis lazuli, jasper, onyx — plus cultured pearls and freshwater varieties. Touch them before you choose.

🔮

Crystals & Findings

Swarovski-style crystals, glass beads, seed beads, and a full range of jewelry findings — clasps, jump rings, wire, chain, and tools for every level of craftsperson.

🛠️

Jewelry Repair

Professional in-store repair services: ring resizing, chain repair and soldering, stone replacement, clasp repair, and restringing. Handled by experienced jewelers who care about your piece.

⚖️

Gold & Silver Buying

We buy gold and silver — broken jewelry, loose pieces, old chains. Fair assessments done in-store. Bring in what you have and walk out with cash the same day.
