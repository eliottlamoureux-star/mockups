Our Services

Industrial-Grade Electrical Solutions
for Every Project Phase

From pre-construction planning to final commissioning, our CCQ-certified teams handle every stage of your commercial electrical project.

New Construction Wiring

Full electrical systems for commercial builds and industrial facilities — from conduit runs and switchgear installation to distribution panels and fire alarm circuits. We coordinate directly with your GC to keep the build on schedule.

Panel Upgrades & Generators

Upgrade your facility's electrical capacity with modern panels rated for industrial loads. We size, install, and commission standby generators — including automatic transfer switches — so your operations never go dark.

Energy Audits & Optimization

Reduce your facility's electrical costs with a comprehensive energy audit. We analyze load profiles, identify inefficiencies, recommend LED retrofits and power factor corrections, and deliver a detailed report with ROI projections.
